Project Overview

The Problem
Non-professional caregivers who care for the elderly, disabled or otherwise dependant family member are often isolated and overwhelmed in their roles due to the inherent lack of support structure. These caregivers need more access to tools to help them find support, get advice and share resources.

The Solution
Design an App that would provide access to dedicated forums, connect with caregivers in similar circumstances, or professional for expert advice. Include features that would help them set self-care goals and a schedule for loved one’s needs that can be shared with hired caregivers or family members to assist in providing seamless care when they are unable to physically be with their loved one.
